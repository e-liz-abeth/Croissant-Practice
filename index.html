A croissant is a buttery, flaky, French viennoiserie pastry inspired by the shape of the utrian Kipferl but using the French yeast=leavened laminated dough. Croissants are named for their historical crescent shape, the dough is layer with butter, rolled and folded several times in succession, then rolled into a thin sheet, in a technique called laminating. It results in a delicious buttery pastry that can be enjoyed during anytime of the day.
